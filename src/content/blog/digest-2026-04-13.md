## A Single Binary Operator Can Compute Every Elementary Function

**[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.21852)**

Boolean logic needs only NAND gates to build everything. Math never had that. Until now: eml(x,y) = exp(x) - ln(y) generates constants (e, π, i), arithmetic (add, multiply, exponentiate), and transcendentals (sin, cos, sqrt, log). Every scientific calculator function becomes a binary tree of identical eml() nodes. The researcher found it through exhaustive search, then proved it works by demonstrating gradient-based symbolic regression can recover exact formulas from data when the generating law is elementary. Grammar: S → 1 | eml(S,S). That's it.

## South Korea: API Trading Now 30% of Crypto Market Volume

**[Cointelegraph](https://cointelegraph.com/news/south-korea-api-crypto-trading-30-percent-manipulation-risk)**

## Crypto ETPs Record $1.1B Weekly Inflows, Best Since January

**[Cointelegraph](https://cointelegraph.com/news/crypto-etp-1-1-billion-inflows-strongest-gains-january)**
