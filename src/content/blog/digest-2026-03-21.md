## Grayscale files for HYPE ETF tracking largest onchain perps DEX Hyperliquid

[The Block](https://www.theblock.co/post/342567/grayscale-files-for-hype-etf-tracking-largest-onchain-perps-dex-hyperliquid)

Grayscale joined Bitwise and 21Shares in filing S-1s for Hyperliquid ETFs, targeting the leading onchain perpetuals DEX. This marks the first major ETF push for a pure DeFi protocol rather than L1/L2 infrastructure—if approved, it would give TradFi exposure to actual decentralized trading volume instead of just speculative tokens.

## Lawmakers reach breakthrough with 'agreement in principle' over stablecoin yield in sweeping crypto bill

[The Block](https://www.theblock.co/post/342594/lawmakers-reach-breakthrough-with-agreement-in-principle-over-stablecoin-yield-in-sweeping-crypto-bill)

The CLARITY Act stalemate over stablecoin yield distribution finally cracked after months of gridlock between the White House and congressional factions. How issuers can share interest with holders was the last major holdup—this deal could unblock the entire regulatory package and set the framework for yield-bearing stablecoins in the U.S.

## Solana Foundation president says crypto gaming is dead

[The Block](https://www.theblock.co/post/342578/solana-foundation-president-says-crypto-gaming-is-dead)

Not mincing words: the Solana Foundation's president declared the crypto gaming wave finished, calling out token-first games that prioritized extraction over gameplay. This is a major ecosystem player admitting what's been obvious for a while—most Web3 games shipped broken incentives wrapped in NFT hype, and the model collapsed under its own weight.

## Ledger hires ex-Circle exec as CFO, opens NYC office to expand US business

[Cointelegraph](https://cointelegraph.com/news/ledger-hires-ex-circle-exec-as-cfo-opens-nyc-office-to-expand-us-business)

Ledger brought on a former Circle CFO and established a New York presence as it gears up for U.S. market expansion beyond consumer hardware wallets. The institutional custody and enterprise play is real—this signals a pivot toward regulated financial clients who need compliance-friendly cold storage at scale.

## Job Cuts at Crypto Firms Are Being Linked to AI Integration, Potentially Masking Routine Layoffs

[Decrypt](https://decrypt.co/329847/job-cuts-at-crypto-firms-are-being-linked-to-ai-integration-potentially-masking-routine-layoffs)

Multiple crypto companies blamed recent layoffs on AI efficiency gains, but the timing and scale suggest these were standard cost-cutting moves rebranded with a tech narrative. Attributing headcount reductions to automation sounds better than "we overhired during the bull run"—and it's getting harder to tell which is which.

## Coinbase Rolls Out Stock Perpetual Futures for International Users

[Decrypt](https://decrypt.co/329823/coinbase-rolls-out-stock-perpetual-futures-for-international-users)

Coinbase launched stock perps for users outside the U.S., letting international traders get leveraged exposure to equities through crypto derivatives. This blurs the line between crypto exchanges and traditional brokerages—expect regulators to notice when retail traders start treating Coinbase like a TradFi platform that happens to settle in USDC.
