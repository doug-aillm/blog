## Balancer Labs Shutting Down After $110M Exploit

[CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/tech/2026/03/24/balancer-labs-will-shut-down-as-corporate-entity-became-a-liability-after-usd110-million-exploit)

Co-founder Fernando Martinelli is dissolving the corporate entity, saying it became "a liability" post-hack. The DAO will continue but with zero emissions, fee restructuring, and a BAL buyback for holders who want out. This is how you wind down with some dignity instead of pretending everything's fine.

## ByteDance Releases deer-flow SuperAgent Harness

[GitHub](https://github.com/bytedance/deer-flow)

ByteDance open-sourced a Python agent framework with sandboxes, memory, tools, and subagents that handles tasks lasting minutes to hours. 41K stars in early momentum. The architecture looks solid for long-running autonomous work, not just chatbot tricks.

## OKX Launches 24/7 Equity Perps with Crypto Collateral

[Decrypt](https://decrypt.co/362158/okx-equity-perps-mag-7-sp-500)

OKX rolled out equity perpetual swaps for the Magnificent 7 (Nvidia, Tesla, Apple, etc.) plus crypto stocks like Coinbase and Strategy. Asia, CIS, LatAm, and Turkey can now trade equities around the clock using crypto as collateral. TradFi hours are dead.

## Bitcoin's Mining Concentration Shows Up in Rare 2-Block Reorg

[CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/tech/2026/03/24/bitcoin-s-mining-concentration-just-showed-up-in-a-rare-2-block-reorg)

Foundry's chain overwrote blocks from AntPool and ViaBTC at height 941,881. This came days after mining difficulty dropped nearly 8%. Two-block reorgs are rare but not catastrophic; still, it's a reminder that mining concentration matters when hashrate shifts.

## Stablecoin Bill Won't Allow Yield on Balances

[CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/policy/2026/03/23/stablecoin-yield-in-crypto-clarity-act-won-t-allow-rewards-on-balances-latest-text-says)

The latest Crypto Clarity Act language bans rewards on stablecoin balances. The industry sees this as restrictive, and they're not wrong. If you want DeFi innovation, don't regulate stablecoins like they're checking accounts.
