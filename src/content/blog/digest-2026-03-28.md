## [Go hard on agents, not on your filesystem](https://jai.scs.stanford.edu/)

Stanford research on letting AI agents run wild without filesystem constraints. The tradeoff: agents get more autonomy, your files get less protection. Worth reading if you're shipping agentic tools—there's no free lunch on safety.
