When a $8T AUM firm adds spot crypto alongside equities, that's not a sideshow—it's infrastructure reaching critical mass.

## AI & Dev Tools

**[Show HN: Tiny LLM to Demystify Language Models](https://github.com/arman-bd/guppylm)** — Someone built GuppyLM, a minimal language model to teach how LLMs actually work. The best way to understand neural nets is to implement one from scratch. This does that without the noise.

**[Real-Time AI (Audio/Video In, Voice Out) on M3 Pro](https://github.com/fikrikarim/parlor)** — Parlor runs real-time multimodal AI on an M3 Pro with Gemma E2B—audio and video input, voice output, all local. Edge inference keeps getting faster, and the gap between cloud and local is shrinking every quarter.

**[Gemma 4 Running on iPhone](https://apps.apple.com/nl/app/google-ai-edge-gallery/id6749645337)** — Google shipped Gemma 4 as a native iOS app. Mobile AI isn't a toy anymore—it's a deployment target with real constraints and real users. If your model can't run on-device, you're designing for yesterday's infrastructure.
