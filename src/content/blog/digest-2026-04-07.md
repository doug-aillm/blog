## AI & Dev

**[LLMs May Be Standardizing Human Expression](https://dornsife.usc.edu/news/stories/ai-may-be-making-us-think-and-write-more-alike/)** — USC researchers argue that billions using the same handful of chatbots is shrinking cognitive diversity worldwide. When LLM outputs favor Western educated perspectives and linear reasoning over intuition, entire societies lose problem-solving approaches that might have worked better.

**[Apollo 11 Bug Found After 57 Years](https://www.juxt.pro/blog/a-bug-on-the-dark-side-of-the-moon/)** — A resource lock in the Apollo Guidance Computer's gyro code leaks when the IMU is caged mid-torque, silently disabling platform realignment. The code was scrutinized by thousands, emulated instruction-by-instruction, and formally verified—but behavioral specification modeling (via Allium) found a missing four-byte cleanup that would have left Michael Collins unable to align for rendezvous behind the Moon.

**[Every GPU That Mattered](https://sheets.works/data-viz/every-gpu)** — Interactive timeline of 49 graphics cards across 30 years, from Quake to Cyberpunk. The gap between what flagship buyers chase ($1,999 RTX 5090 at 0.42% Steam share) and what gamers actually use ($329 RTX 3060 at 4.1%) is the GPU market in one chart.

## Crypto & Finance

**[Circle's Arc Network Plans Post-Quantum Signatures](https://decrypt.co/363395/circle-arc-network-quantum-resistance-bitcoin-ethereum-face-threat)** — Arc, Circle's EVM-compatible L1, will launch with post-quantum signature support as part of a broader roadmap to protect institutional assets from quantum attacks. While Bitcoin and Ethereum debate migration timelines, Arc is shipping the defense layer from day one.

**[IMF: Tokenized Finance Could Amplify Crises](https://decrypt.co/363379/imf-warns-tokenized-finance-stablecoins-could-amplify-financial-crises)** — The IMF warns that stablecoins behave more like money market funds than actual money and could face confidence-driven runs as tokenization scales. Traditional finance relies on settlement delays that give regulators breathing room during crises—instant atomic settlement removes the brake pedal.

**[Solo Bitcoin Miner Hits $210K Block](https://decrypt.co/363396/solo-bitcoin-miner-beats-the-odds-scores-210k-block-reward)** — Running 230 TH/s (0.00002% of global hashrate), a solo miner validated block 943,411 and earned 3.139 BTC. Lottery odds, but someone wins. The decentralization edge case that keeps the ideal alive.
