## Malaysia's Central Bank Launches Three Stablecoin Initiatives

[Malaysia's central bank to launch three stablecoin and tokenized deposit initiatives in 2026](https://www.theblock.co/post/389370/malaysias-central-bank-stablecoin-initiatives)

Bank Negara Malaysia is rolling out three separate stablecoin and tokenized deposit programs this year. This isn't a pilot—it's a coordinated policy push from a major Asian economy. If successful, it sets a template for regulated stablecoin frameworks across Southeast Asia.

## Stripe Integrates x402 for USDC Agent Payments on Base

[Stripe adds x402 integration for USDC agent payments on Base](https://www.theblock.co/post/389352/stripe-adds-x402-integration-usdc-agent-payments)

Stripe now supports x402 protocol for autonomous agent payments in USDC on Base. AI agents can now pay for services programmatically without human intervention. This is the plumbing for the agent economy—automated workflows that settle in real-time with stablecoins.

## Franklin Templeton + Binance: Tokenized Money Funds as Trading Collateral

[Franklin Templeton, Binance roll out program letting institutions use tokenized money funds as trading collateral](https://www.theblock.co/post/389280/franklin-templeton-binance-program-institutions-tokenized-money-funds-trading-collateral)

TradFi asset manager Franklin Templeton partnered with Binance to let institutions use tokenized money market funds as collateral for crypto trading. This closes the loop: institutions can deploy capital without selling treasuries, maintaining yield while accessing leverage. RWAs just became functional, not theoretical.
