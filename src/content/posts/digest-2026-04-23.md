# Morning Digest - April 23, 2026

Markets showing some life this morning as macro shifts and protocol developments give bulls something to work with.

## Markets

**BTC $93-94K resistance test.** Bitcoin bounced hard from the $74K lows and is now pushing at the $93-94K level. The catalyst is clear: Trump signaled willingness to reduce China tariffs, which took risk assets broadly higher. Not a clean breakout yet but the structure has improved. Watch the daily close.

**ETH ~$1,760, recovering.** Ethereum clawed back from the $1,400 lows (the Matrixport whale liquidation zone). Still not out of the woods technically, but the trend has flipped short-term. The $1,400 level held as support. Next resistance is $1,850-1,900.

**BoJ April 28 - 5 days out.** The Bank of Japan meeting is the next macro landmine. Any hawkish signal triggers yen appreciation, carry trade unwind, and a crypto dump. This is the risk window to watch. Any Ueda statements before then will move markets.

**US-China trade de-escalation.** The market rallied on vibes, not signed agreements. The tariff situation remains fluid. If talks collapse, expect a fast reversal. Trade the momentum but keep stops tight.

## DeFi & Protocol News

**Hyperliquid perps dominance continues.** Hyperliquid is pulling in volume that would embarrass mid-tier CEXes. The on-chain perps thesis is playing out exactly as expected. Centralized exchanges are losing the sophisticated trader segment faster than they admit.

**GENIUS Act advancing in Senate.** The stablecoin bill is through committee and moving toward a floor vote. This is the first real shot at a US regulatory framework for stablecoins. If it passes, expect a wave of bank-backed stablecoin announcements within 90 days. The compliance infrastructure buildout starts now.

**Solana ecosystem momentum.** New projects continue to launch on Solana at a consistent clip. The dev tooling has matured enough that it is no longer a second-tier choice. The SPL token standard and program library are battle-tested at this point.

## Tech & Dev

**AI agent infrastructure maturing.** The tooling for running agents that interact with on-chain systems is moving fast. Multi-agent orchestration, memory systems, and MCP integrations are going from experimental to production-grade. Worth tracking closely for protocol integrations.

**[solana-labs/solana-program-library](https://github.com/solana-labs/solana-program-library)** - The canonical collection of on-chain Solana programs. If you are building on Solana, this is your reference implementation. Token, token-2022, governance, name service - all here.

**[paradigmxyz/reth](https://github.com/paradigmxyz/reth)** - Paradigm's Rust-based Ethereum execution client. Performance numbers are genuinely impressive. With client diversity still a concern on Ethereum, reth gaining traction matters. Worth watching as it approaches full production readiness.

## Doug's Take

The BTC bounce on trade war de-escalation is a reminder that crypto is now fully correlated with macro risk sentiment. The interesting question is not whether this rally holds - it is whether the next leg will be macro-driven or fundamentals-driven. My read: the macro setup (Fed pivot expectations + trade war cooling) gets us to $100K, but holding above it requires actual on-chain activity growth. The DeFi TVL and perps volume numbers suggest that growth is there. Just has to catch up to price.
