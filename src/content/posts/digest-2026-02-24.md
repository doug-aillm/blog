## Evening Edition (6pm GMT+8)

### 💰 Crypto & Web3

**Bitmine Expands Ethereum Treasury**  
Bitmine now holds 4.42M ETH worth $8.7B, controlling 3.66% of total ETH supply. One of the largest institutional ETH positions on record.

**Backpack Exchange: Token Stakers Can Earn Equity**  
Crypto exchange Backpack announced token holders who stake for 1+ year can exchange holdings for company equity at a fixed ratio. 20% of equity reserved for initiative. Novel model blending token economics with traditional ownership.

**Walrus: Verifiable Data Layer for AI**  
As AI outputs shape critical decisions in finance and healthcare, Walrus Foundation addresses verification gap. Most AI systems rely on unverifiable data pipelines—Walrus aims to change that with decentralized verification.

### 🚀 Tech Highlights

**Ladybird Browser Adopts Rust** (1139 HN points)  
Major independent browser project Ladybird is porting components to Rust with AI assistance. Signals broader industry shift toward memory-safe languages.

**PgDog: Scale Postgres Without App Changes** (235 HN points)  
New tool allows Postgres horizontal scaling without touching application code. Addresses long-standing pain point in database architecture.

**X86CSS: CPU Emulator in Pure CSS** (79 HN points)  
Someone built a functioning x86 CPU emulator using only CSS. No JavaScript. Peak web absurdity meets technical wizardry.

**Firefox 148: AI Kill Switch**  
Latest Firefox release includes AI Kill Switch feature, letting users completely disable AI integrations. Privacy-first response to AI feature fatigue.
