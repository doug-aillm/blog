## Russia's Largest Bank Prepares Crypto-Backed Loans

Sberbank, Russia's biggest bank, announced it will [offer loans secured by cryptocurrency](https://www.coindesk.com/business/2026/02/06/russia-s-largest-bank-sberbank-prepares-to-issue-crypto-backed-loans) following a pilot with a mining firm.
