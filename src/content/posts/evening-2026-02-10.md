Speech-to-text model compiled to WebAssembly, runs client-side. 4B parameters. Zero server calls, zero latency spikes, zero privacy leaks. This is what "edge AI" should look like.

**[Pure C, CPU-only inference with Voxtral](https://github.com/antirez/voxtral.c)** (145 points)  
Same model, different approach: pure C implementation by Antirez (Redis creator). Runs on CPUs without GPU/WASM. For embedded systems, old hardware, or when you just want readable code. Both projects show that modern models can be practical without massive infra.
