## 🌅 Tuesday Morning Briefing - March 3, 2026

### 📊 Crypto & DeFi Headlines (Overnight US/EU Markets)

1. **Bitcoin's 5% Spike Driven by Short-Covering, Not Fresh Buying**  
   Monday's BTC rally to $69K+ was fueled primarily by short liquidations rather than new spot demand. Market data shows rising open interest with large liquidation clusters around $65K and $70K, suggesting the rally may be fragile without stronger buying pressure.

2. **Vitalik Buterin Unveils Plan to Curb Ethereum Block Builder Centralization**  
   Ethereum's co-founder proposes "big FOCIL" and encrypted mempools to prevent centralization in the block building pipeline. The plan also targets "toxic MEV" where traders front-run or sandwich users' trades.

3. **Tom Lee's Bitmine Boosts Ether Holdings to 4.47M Tokens with $98M ETH Purchase**  
   The ether treasury firm now controls nearly $10B in assets and over $6B of staked ETH. Bitmine's latest acquisition signals continued institutional confidence in Ethereum despite 60% drawdown from 2025 highs.

4. **Strategy Purchased More Than $200M in Bitcoin Last Week**  
   Michael Saylor's company added 3,015 BTC at $67,700 per coin in its 101st purchase, lifting total holdings to 720,737 BTC valued at $47B+. This marks another buy below cost basis, funded through stock sales.

5. **HyperLiquid Became Retail's Bear Market Playground**  
   While Bitcoin and Ether remain in bear markets, HYPE token has climbed alongside gold. HyperLiquid's derivatives volume expanded with weekend equity trading gaining traction among retail traders seeking volatility.

6. **Crypto World Faces Growing Pressure to Relent on Stablecoin Rewards**  
   Bank lobbyists' campaign against stablecoin yield landed a significant blow in Washington. As much as crypto negotiators paint it as unfair, one dramatic concession could unlock passage of broader market structure legislation.

7. **Nasdaq Files for Prediction-Style Binary Options on Nasdaq-100 Index**  
   Following Cboe's lead, Nasdaq seeks SEC approval to list yes-or-no bets on the NQ-100 amid continued demand for prediction markets. The exchange's filing signals Wall Street's embrace of prediction market mechanics.

8. **Turkey's Ruling Party Proposes 10% Crypto Income Tax**  
   The bill proposes a 10% tax on gains from regulated crypto platforms, withheld quarterly. The president would have authority to adjust rates between 0% and 20%, creating flexible regulatory framework.

9. **South Korea Investigates Seed Phrase Leak in Photo Leading to $4.8M Crypto Theft**  
   National Tax Service shared a photo intended to showcase crypto seizure but accidentally exposed seed phrases. Attackers drained $4.8M before authorities detected the breach.

10. **Iranian Crypto Outflows Jump 700% Minutes After U.S.-Israeli Airstrikes**  
    Blockchain analytics firm Elliptic reports flows from Iran's largest exchange spiked immediately after strikes on Tehran, pointing to possible capital flight as geopolitical tensions escalate.
