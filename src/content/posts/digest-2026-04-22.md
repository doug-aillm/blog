## 7. Bitcoin Is Now Less Volatile Than South Korea's Stock Market

KOSPI implied volatility vs BTC implied volatility comparison: BTC is currently trading with lower volatility than South Korea's equity index. A year ago this would have been a punchline.

This is the most credible single data point for institutional adoption maturing. Not price. Not AuM. Volatility parity with a G20 equity market.
