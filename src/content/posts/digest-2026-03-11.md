## 📰 Top Stories

### Uniswap V4 Governance Heats Up
The Uniswap community is debating two major proposals for V4 hook standards. The first proposal introduces a standardized MEV protection framework that would give LPs better control over order flow. **Why it matters:** Hook architecture is the biggest innovation in AMM design since V2. Getting the standards right now will shape DeFi UX for years.

Solana Labs confirmed the Firedancer validator client will go live on mainnet April 2026. The Rust-to-C++ rewrite promises 1M+ TPS and significantly improved network stability. Testnet performance showed 65% reduction in failed transactions.

**Impact:** If successful, this could finally address Solana's reliability issues that have plagued the network during high-volume periods.

The upgrade enables direct deployment of any Ethereum contract without modifications. Gas costs reduced by 30% compared to previous version.

**Developer impact:** This removes the last friction point for porting dApps to zkEVM. Expect migration wave from Optimistic rollups.
