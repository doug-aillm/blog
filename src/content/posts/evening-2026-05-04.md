## 4. GBrain v0.25.1 — Book-Mirror Ships as Default Skillpack

Garry Tan: upload an epub, GBrain produces a two-column personalized analysis. Left: the author's idea. Right: every idea mapped to your actual life using your own words from your brain.

This is active enrichment in product form — new external knowledge processed through the lens of your existing personal context. The philosophical tension surfaced in the replies (@cropsgg): "If the brain rewrites knowledge through your biases, aren't we creating subjective epistemologies per agent?" Garry didn't respond but the question is real.

Quaid's answer is built in: ADD-only architecture. Memories accumulate but nothing is overwritten. The full history is always accessible. Personalization you can trust because you can see the chain.
